What is PeerPressure?

PeerPressure is a website which is based on peer collaboration. Students are able to create posts regarding any subject up for discussion such as: homework problems, school events, or study groups. The possibilities are endless! After this other students can respond to the original post, adding additional information, answering a question, or just providing feedback. This peer-to-peer communication empowers students to help their fellow classmates and encourages teamwork and collaboration.

                       

Features

  • Unique user accounts - Individual accounts for each student
  • Create posts - Topics are unlimited
  • Edit posts - Change your question or your responses
  • Delete posts - Get rid of invalid information
  • Reply to posts - Collaboration between students
  • Posts show creator and when it was created
  • Ask questions and get answers 24/7 - Not just in the classroom

Design


  1. Easy to read posts identifying when and by whom the post was created

  2. Edit and delete functionality

  3. Reply to another user's post
