How to use PeerPressure


New Users:

  • Click 'Sign Up' on the PeerPressure homepage.

  • Enter the required information into the appropriate fields

  • Confirm your account information and click 'Sign Up'


Continuing Users:

  • To add a new post: Click 'Add Post' on the top of the page. Enter the title of the post and the content of the post then click 'Post'. You should see your newly added post at the top of the posts with your username and date of submission.

  • To edit a post: Users can only edit posts which they are the original author of. To make an edit, click the pencil icon on the bottom right of the post. You can now edit your post to your liking and once satisfied, press 'Edit'. The post's content will be changed and the post will now present the time of the edit.

  • To delete a post: Users can only delete posts which they are the original author of. To delete a post, click the trash icon on the bottom right of the post. This will immediately remove the post from the post listings and all of its replies.

  • To reply to a post: Users can only reply to another user's post. To reply to a post, click the reply or arrow on the bottom right of a post, and enter your message in the textfield that appears. If you see a caret-down icon on the bottom right of a post that means that there are replies to that post. Click the caret-down icon to expand the replies for that particular post. Click the caret-up icon to hide the replies for that particular post.
